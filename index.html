🦅 Sovereign Origin — We The People

The indigenous peoples of this continent created the first constitution in North American history — the Gayanashagowa (Great Law of Peace) of the Haudenosaunee Confederacy, centuries before 1787. Benjamin Franklin, John Adams and the Founding Fathers studied and were directly inspired by this indigenous democratic system. The phrase "We the People" echoes the sovereign governance that indigenous nations practiced for millennia. We are the original sovereign peoples. We wrote the first constitution. We are still here.
